  • The rates are exclusive of Luxury tax and Service Tax.
  • 50% booking fee is needed to confirm the booking within 24 hours and is strictly non-refundable.
  • ID proof copies (passport , election card, pancard, driving license) is mandatory on arrival of all individuals staying at the premises.
  • These prices are subject to change.
  • Paying Online via Credit Card: Payment will be requested in USD and a standard transfer/bank fee of 5% USD will be applicable.
  • The minimum stay at any of our villas is 3 nights. During public/bank holidays is 4 nights and Over X'mas/New Year is 7 nights.
  • Our prices are based on the number of adults + children staying at the villa. There is no charge for children under 5 years old.
  • If children require an extra bed/mattress then an extra cost will be applicable. Please specify the ages of children at time of travel. Proof of age of children is required whilst booking to avail discount.
  • Extra individuals will strictly NOT be allowed within the premises even for few minutes. The booking is only for the guests who have paid for the facilities and submitted their IDs upon check in.
  • Any extra individual (including children above 5 years) found staying in the villa without our notice, full security deposit will be forfeited without discussion.
  • No Smoking inside the villa/bedrooms. If found we may ask you to leave the premises and security deposit will be forfeited without discussion.
