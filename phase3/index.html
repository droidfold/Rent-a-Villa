SFX Holiday Home - 3 Bedrooms Private Villa with Swimming Pool

About SFXHolidayHomes

Set in the very heart of Calangute, located on Adventure Street this luxury furnished villa offers has plenty of space and is ideal for families, couples or friends looking for a tranquil and relaxing holiday. Inside, the appreciation of original features and hand carved furniture is enhanced by the tasteful use of decoration, whilst ensuring maximum comfort for its guests. A strong authentic ambiance can be felt throughout the villa, and with magnificent, breath-taking views of the swimming pool and kid’s pool this is truly paradise. A 10 minute walk will reach you to Calangute beach.

3 Bedrooms

Accommodate 9 people, with 3 AC bedrooms, upstairs - two twin bedrooms and a double en-suite bedroom on the ground floor, three extra mattresses are available upon request. All your comforts have been thought about with meshed insect blinds, international plug sockets, ceiling fans, seating, wardrobes, mirrors and bedside tables.

2 Bathrooms

Features 2 luxurious modern bathrooms with ceramic tiles on the walls and floor giving the space added panache. The open design provides a more integrated space and allows for easy access. Features include a curb-less, door-less shower, soft closing toilet seats and large counter to hold place all your essentials. The blue master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom. The green and pink bedroom have a common bathroom.

Kitchen

With the rich, traditional colours of the old country, this Goan style kitchen make an inviting environment for both cooking and conversing. The kitchen is fully furnished with a fridge, freezer, kettle, toaster, microwave, 4 ring gas stove with oven, cooking utensils, glassware, crockery and cutlery.

Living Room

The villa has modern sophisticated decor, and incorporates the work of local artists. The living room opens out onto a covered terrace leading into the garden. The living room has a comfortable L shaped sofa for 10 people with a coffee table. Television with Tata Sky and board games are also provided. The superb dining room also seats up to 6 people and leads into the kitchen. To maximise outdoor living on your holiday, a pretty covered pergola shades the sitting facilities, which overlooks the landscaped gardens.

Garden

The whole villa is immaculately kept as are the surrounding gardens. The charming garden is very pretty and well-tended and stocked with fruit trees. A large lawn area around the swimming pool provides for ample space to lounge around, sunbathe and offers the kids an opportunity to run about. The villa has an outdoor-seated area situated under a wood fashioned pergola - ideal for those poolside BBQ's!

Swimming Pool

The pool is located in front of the villa and the high compound wall offers a degree of privacy as well as a peaceful retreat, and there's even a shaded terrace to escape the midday sun. The 36 metre squared swimming pool consists of an 8 metre squared kid’s pool. The dilemma is whether to hang around the pool or take a 10 minute walk to the sea and beach! Pool timing are from 8am to 8pm.

Housekeeping

House Keeping is done daily from 2pm-5pm. Linen and towels are changed twice a week. We have provided you with lodging and cooking facilities with no meals. Cooking can be provided for and additional cost, please pay the housekeeper directly, whose charges are reasonable. Stays longer than 3 nights will receive a welcome pack.

Directions to SFX Holiday Home

From the airport/train station ask the taxi driver to take you to Calangute, North Goa. Villa Calangute SFXHolidayHomes is located on Adventure Street. As you enter Calangute via Chogm Road, you will see St. Alex Church at the roundabout. Take left at the roundabout, then 1st left again (this road leads towards the old I95 and art chamber gallery). You will pass Azzure Resort on the left hand side. Villa Calangute Phase 6 is located on the right hand side of the road, opposite a large house which has two lions outside. Continue down the road and you will see 2 identical villas on the right hand side. The first villa is phase 4 then the next villa is SFX Holiday Homes villa, there are 2 lions on the entrance gate. Each villa has a signboard on the gate. Please click the link and print the map http://www.SFXHolidayHomes.com/map.pdf

You can also come to Kamat Holiday Homes Phase 2 and behind this resort you will pass Renzo Guest house and then take a left you will see Lourenco Guest house followed with a sign board of Villa Calangute SFX Holiday Homes,.
