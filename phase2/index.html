SFX 3bhk Portuguese Goan House

About SFX 3bhk Portuguese Goan House

For a group getaway that’s close to the entertainment strip and lined with eateries, this 6 bedroom house is ideal. The architectural balance and beautifully designed interiors and gardens offer the sophisticated traveller a genuine close up experience of Goan hospitality. A 2 minute walk to Calangute Beach, self-catered kitchen, and friendly staff are other potential box tickers while the property’s vast garden is great for the kids to run around.

2 Bedrooms

All the bedrooms have elegant French windows which lead onto a large balcony. The balcony has comfortable seating where you can sit down and soak in the sea breeze. The blue master bedroom and pink bedroom each have a king size bed whereas the other bedrooms have twin beds (which can come together as a double). All bedrooms have air conditioning. One bedroom is located on the ground floor so is ideal for people with particular mobility requirements.

1 Bathroom

Each contain a hand basin, vanity mirror, western style WC and shower with hot and cold water. The blue master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom. The downstairs bathroom is next to the lemon bedroom. The green and pink bedroom have a common bathroom.

Kitchen

The kitchen diner is the multi-functional hub of the house, where the family can congregate to cook and converse. Self-catering offers you with the flexibility to do it yourself, you aren’t restricted to designated meal times. If you want to have a lie in, you can! Wake up at a time that suits you and rustle up a delicious breakfast in your own time or head out and treat yourself to brunch in a local cafe.

Living Room

Situated on the ground floor and opens out onto a covered terrace leading into the garden. Seating is provided on the front porch. The living room has a 3 seater sofa, 2 armchairs and a coffee table. Television with tata sky, books, board games and magazines are also provided.

Garden

The whole villa is immaculately kept as are the surrounding gardens. The charming garden is very pretty and well-tended and stocked with fruit trees. The large lawn area provides lots of space to run around or just sit back and relax on the sun beds. The separate patio area is ideal for a BBQ.

Swimming Pool

The pool is located in between both villas and the high compound wall offers a degree of privacy as well as a peaceful retreat. The pool is shared with PHASE1 villa next door. The pool size is 9 mtrs by 5 mtrs consisting of a 2 by 5 kid’s pool. The dilemma is whether to hang around the pool or take a 2 minute walk to the sea and beach! Pool timing are from 8am to 8pm.

Housekeeping

House Keeping is done daily from 2pm-5pm. Linen and towels are changed twice a week. We have provided you with lodging and cooking facilities with no meals. Cooking can be provided for and additional cost, please pay the housekeeper directly, whose charges are reasonable. Stays longer than 3 nights will receive a welcome pack.

Directions to PHASE2

From the airport/train station ask the taxi driver to take you to Calangute, North Goa. Take the road towards Kamat Holiday Homes Resort Phase 1, continue down the road until you pass Baskin-Robbins on the right hand side. You will see a travel kiosk called Day Trippers and Calangute Mall on the left hand side, take a right turn here to Holiday Street, the road is leading to Hotel Goan Heritage. Once you drive down Holiday Street, take the turning between Villa Calangute phase 1 and Ondas Do Mar Beach Resort towards hotel Grandeur De Sanchi. Phase 2 is located on the right hand side of the road.
