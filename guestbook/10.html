  Peaceful, Professional, Courteous And Well Located

Review posted on TripAdvisor.co.uk on 06/05/2013 for a stay in April 2013

I've been going to Goa for years and have lived all over (including my own property there). As there were 6 of us travelling, we came across Villa Calangute online and decided to try it.

They use a simple and straightforward booking system which is very transparent and helpful. The option of having a shopping list ready for you when you arrive is an added convenience.

Lucinda got in touch with us before we arrived and was available to guide us to the Villa once we landed in Goa. Check in and 'orientation' (where she explained the basic functioning of the villa) were a breeze and handled well. We had a housekeeper (Roseann) present daily from 9am to 5pm and she was very courteous and unobtrusive, not to mention really helpful.

The villa itself (we stayed in Phase 4) was immaculate, clean and welcoming. Tastefully decorated and very comfortable. We met with Dexter at the start and the end of our stay - his hospitable nature is clearly evident and directly linked to the experience provided.

Our holiday was a great de-stresser overall and Villa Calangute played a central role in that. I would most definitely be staying with them again and would highly recommend them to anyone planning a visit to Goa.

Manu --Mumbai/Pune
