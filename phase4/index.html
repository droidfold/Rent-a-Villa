SFX Holiday Homes - Luxury Villa in Calangute with Swimming Pool

About SFX Holiday Homes

Situated just 10 minutes from the beautiful beach of Calangute, and surrounded by beautiful coconut trees this upmarket gated accommodation features a self-catered kitchen, a landscaped swimming pool and kid’s pool. Set within large grounds, the villa is comfortably furnished to a high standard, the villa combines modern facilities including air conditioning, whilst retaining plenty of original rustic charm with traditional wooden teak furniture and open plan living.

3 Bedrooms

The green master bedroom has a double bed whereas the other bedrooms have twin beds (which can come together as a double). Each of the 3 bedrooms is furnished with a ceiling fan, seating, wardrobe, mirror and bedside table. This 3 bedroom villa with a swimming pool comprises of 1 bedroom on the ground floor and 2 on the 1st floor. Towels and linens are provided. We also provide blankets, so you’ll be enveloped, cosseted and cosy, no matter what the season -- there’s also air conditioning to keep you cool in the summer.

2 Bathrooms

Each contain hand basin, vanity mirror, western style WC and shower with hot and cold water. The master bedroom has an en-suite bathroom. The lemon and pink bedroom have a shared bathroom.

Kitchen

Is fully equipped with a range of crockery and utensils and other appliances such as a fridge, freezer, washing machine and kettle to name a few. Other facilities include iron and ironing board, BBQ set (on request), toaster, microwave and 4 ring gas stove with oven.

Living Room

On the ground floor opens out onto a covered terrace leading into the garden. The living room combine interior comforts with refreshing garden views with its L shaped sofa, coffee table, board games and LCD TV screen with Tata Sky, this area is just a perfect haven to relax. The living room area provides easy access to the poolside – perfect for a quick dip on those hot summer days!

Garden

The mature gardens are another feature, and have a range of furniture including patio coffee table & sofas and a swimming pool with children’s pool, plus parking for 4 cars. A large lawn area around the swimming pool provides for ample space to lounge around, sunbathe and offers the kids an opportunity to run about. The pergola is ideal for enjoying al fresco dining on your holiday.

Swimming Pool

The pool is located in front of the villa and the high compound wall offers a degree of privacy as well as a peaceful retreat, and there's even a shaded terrace to escape the midday sun. The 9 metre by 4 meter swimming pool consists of a 4 meter by 2 meter kid's pool. The dilemma is whether to hang around the pool or take a 10 minute walk to the sea and beach! Pool timing are from 8am to 8pm.

Housekeeping

House Keeping is done daily from 2pm-5pm. Linen and towels are changed twice a week. We have provided you with lodging and cooking facilities with no meals. Cooking can be provided for and additional cost, please pay the housekeeper directly, whose charges are reasonable. Stays longer than 3 nights will receive a welcome pack.

Directions to SFX Holiday Homes

From the airport/train station ask the taxi driver to take you to Calangute, North Goa. Villa Calangute Phase 3, 4 and 6 are located on Adventure Street. As you enter Calangute via Chogm Road, you will see St. Alex Church at the roundabout. Take left at the roundabout, then 1st left again (this road leads towards the old I95 and art chamber gallery). You will pass Azzure Resort on the left hand side. Villa Calangute Phase 6 is located on the right hand side of the road, opposite a large house which has two lions outside. Continue down the road and you will see 2 identical villas on the right hand side. The first villa is SFX Holiday Homes then the next villa is phase 3 villa, there are 2 lions on the entrance gate. Each villa has a signboard on the gate. Please click the link and print the map http://www.SFXHolidayHomes.com/map.pdf

You can also come to Kamat Holiday Homes Phase 2 and behind this resort you will pass Renzo Guest house and then take a left you will see Lourenco Guest house followed with a sign board of SFX Holiday Homes.
